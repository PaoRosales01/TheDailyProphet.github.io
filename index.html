HAPPY 26th BIRTHDAY OSCAR!

Special edition of the Daily Prophet: Happy Birthday, Oscar! On this magical and special day, we celebrate the incredible 26th birthday of our beloved Oscar, the prodigious robotics master, fashion wizard, and intrepid traveler. From the intricate circuitry of his robotic creations to the enchantments he embroiders on every thread of his clothing, Oscar has woven a unique world full of technological wonders and magical enchantments. His love for robotics is like a spell, turning every screw and gear into a functional work of art. But oh, let's not forget his devotion to the magical world of Harry Potter. Oscar has proven himself to be a true wizard, bringing the magic of Hogwarts with him to every corner of the world he's explored. And when it comes to travel, Oscar is like a modern nomad, exploring new horizons and taking the essence of fashion with him wherever he goes. His unique style, full of charm and elegance, is as fascinating as the stories he encounters on his travels.

But beyond her incredible skills, Oscar is known for being a pure and generous soul. His goodness and kindness have left an indelible mark on all who have the privilege of knowing him. On this special day, we raise our glasses and wands to toast Oscar, wishing him a year filled with new adventures, discoveries and successes. May the twinkling lights of robotics, the magic of Hogwarts, unexplored destinations and fashion trends guide you in this new chapter of your life. Happy birthday, Oscar, may your path always be illuminated by the magical light emanating from your exceptional self!
